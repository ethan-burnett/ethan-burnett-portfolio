Cluedx Helper

An application to assist and analyse players of Cluedo. The helper works best on desktop/laptop. The main objective was to implement a program which automatically finds deductions within player input. Hence, helping players that utilise the helper by concluding who has and can't have given cards. Also, the application can reconsign contradictions in future player input, once enough information is received. Check it out, I'm hosting it on github right now.

Footsteps

Over 2020 I had the opportunity to develop an application with a team of 9 other students at the University of Canterbury. Footsteps is an activity tracker and finder designed to connect people to their desired activity type and fitness level. We implemented features in a sprint by sprint basis throughout the year, following a scrum agile framework. Footstep is hosted on the UC servers, feel free to check it out.
